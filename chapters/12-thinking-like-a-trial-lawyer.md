# Chapter 12: Thinking Like a Trial Lawyer

The best military lawyers don’t just know rules—they know how to win.

## How Trial Lawyers Think:

- Frame the entire case around one theme.
- Build evidence into a coherent, emotional story.
- Set traps during cross-examination.
- Cross witnesses to expose contradictions, not to argue.

Great trial lawyers prepare their cases like a general plans a war.
